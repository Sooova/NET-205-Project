How We're Solving It

Our technical approach and innovative solutions to transform social media

Cutting through the BS...

At Algora, we’re here to cut through the "profoundly opaque” attention economy, and we’re not here to view our users as a simple money metric (Gorwa & Garton Ash, 2020, p. 20). Our platform seeks to address this systemic problem with our revolutionary approach that places you: whether you’re a viewer, a creator or a business, at the core. We’re all about transparency, whether that be through algorithms, user agency or community governance.

The cornerstone of our solution is complete algorithmic transparency, a principle that is seldom seen in the superficial disclosure practices currently employed by our competitors. We’ve conducted extensive research into how user attitudes towards algorithmic systems, and have found that what the user wants, is complete disclosure of our algorithm’s presence in conjunction with our interface design (Eslami et al., 2019, p. 6).

We’re not here to lock you into black-box systems designed to hook you in and deprive you of your wellbeing. Our transparent recommendation algorithm allows users to access what researchers call “transparent fairness” - where users can understand not just what content you are seeing, but exactly why you are seeing this in the first place (Shin et al., 2024, p. 484).

No longer at the mercy of the dreaded “Algorithm”

Let the content do the talking

As a content creator on Algora, you can say goodbye to the frustrating guesswork that currently dominates platform engagement strategies. Unlike traditional social media platforms where creators attempt to reverse-engineer mysterious algorithmic systems to engage in what resembles "playing the visibility game", Algora provides complete transparency in how content is evaluated and distributed (Cotter, 2019, p. 895). Research shows that influencers often spend considerable time "gathering information about how algorithms function to learn the rules of the game" and view this knowledge gathering as essential "research" (Cotter, 2019, p. 902). Our approach means that you can focus on creating content with the peace of mind that you are no longer at the mercy of the algorithm.

Algorithm Features:

  • • User-configurable recommendation parameters
  • • Real-time explainability for content suggestions
  • • Community-driven algorithm improvements
  • • Privacy-preserving personalization

Cast away the existing dark patterns

Competitor platforms tout algorithmic fairness, whilst intentionally obscuring exactly how their systems work (Katzenbach & Ulbricht, 2019, p. 8). Transparency and fairness cannot be separated, and must operate as “two sides of the same coin” (Shin et al., 2024, p. 483). Algora resolves this existing tension by ensuring our systems are open source, always visible and user adjustable.

What you won't see on Algora

  • • Obscured user metrics
  • • Exploitative engagement strategies
  • • AI content designed to steal your attention

Security & Privacy

Privacy Protection

Unlike traditional social media companies that monetize user data as one of their primary revenue source (Kapczynski, 2020), Algora will never sell, share, or monetize your personal information with third parties. This commitment is built into our non-profit charter and legally binding organizational structure.

  • User-controlled data sharing
  • No third-party data sales

Security Measures

Our systems go above and beyond the industry standard for data security and privacy (Australian Cyber Security Centre, 2025), and will continue to do so.

  • Cryptographically secured data
  • Regular security audits
  • Decentralized infrastructure
  • Bug bounty programs
