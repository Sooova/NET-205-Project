About Us

Meet the team building the future of social media

Our Story

At Algora, we are reimagining social media from the ground up. For many years, online platforms have been built on models that treat user creativity and attention primarily as resources to generate revenue. Every post, like, and share adds value to these systems, yet the individuals who generate this value often receive only minimal recognition in return (Scholz, 2017).

We believe there is a better way. Algora is a non-profit, community-owned social media platform founded on fairness, transparency, and respect for human creativity. Rather than relying on advertising models that profit from user labor, we reward members for the value they create. Instead of leaving users in the dark about how their feeds are curated, Algora provides tools to let members choose and adjust their own algorithm preferences, giving them meaningful control over their online experience (Diakopoulos, 2016). And rather than filling feeds with AI-generated content, we prioritise authentic, human-made connections, which research shows is essential for fostering trust, loyalty, and community engagement (Akgün, 2020; Birhane, 2021).

Algora is an alternative to traditional, profit-driven platforms: a community space where people own the digital infrastructure they use, and where every contribution is valued as part of a collective effort.

Our Mission

Reclaim your feed, own your content, and get paid for your voice. Welcome to Algora, a community-powered social platform designed for transparency, equity, and autonomy—free from algorithmic manipulation and profit-driven AI .

Our Core Values

Reclaiming Value

On today’s platforms, billions of users generate content, moderate communities, and share data that feeds into advertising systems (Scholz, 2017). While this digital labor contributes significantly to platform success, contributors often gain only limited benefits such as metrics, networking opportunities, and a sense of community. Algora exists to redress this imbalance by ensuring users are compensated fairly for the value they generate, while still nurturing intangible benefits like creativity, satisfaction, and connection (Akgün, 2020).

Empowering Choice

Algorithms strongly influence what we see online, but most platforms keep their systems hidden and tuned to maximise engagement, often at the expense of user wellbeing (Diakopoulos, 2016). Algora takes a different approach. Members can choose how their feed is structured — whether in strict chronological order, based on interests they select, or through recommendations curated by trusted peers. Each algorithm is open, transparent, and explained in plain language, so users are always in control.

Protecting Authenticity

Generative AI has made it easy to flood online spaces with synthetic content, risking the dilution of genuine human creativity (Birhane, 2021). Algora commits to a zero-AI content policy: every post and interaction comes from real people and communities, not automated systems designed to manipulate attention.

Building Collective Ownership

As a non-profit, Algora does not exist to enrich shareholders. Instead, it is governed by its community, with users helping to shape policies, features, and future directions. Much like the model pioneered by Wikipedia, important decisions are made collaboratively and democratically, ensuring that every member has a stake in how Algora evolves (Scholz, 2017).

Fostering Transparency

Trust requires openness. At Algora, our data practices, revenue models, and algorithms are fully accessible for review. Members will always know how their data is used, how recommendations are generated, and how resources are allocated. Our commitment is simple: no hidden agendas, no exploitation, no surprises (Diakopoulos, 2016).

Our Principles

1

People Before Profits

Algora is community-owned, not investor-driven. Our goal is to create a digital environment that enhances wellbeing, creativity, and fairness.

2

Digital Labor Deserves Recognition

Content creation, moderation, and community-building are forms of labor. We acknowledge and reward these contributions through fair and transparent systems (Scholz, 2017).

3

Algorithmic Choice and Transparency

You deserve to understand and control how your feed is shaped. Algora offers clear, open-source algorithms you can select and adapt, ensuring your experience is tailored to your needs (Diakopoulos, 2016).

4

Authentic Human Connection

We commit to protecting space for genuine creativity and community by rejecting AI-generated content designed to mimic human interaction (Birhane, 2021). This fosters trust, engagement, and long-term loyalty, as studies in digital marketing show human-centered interactions are more effective in building meaningful relationships (Akgün, 2020).

5

Data Sovereignty

Your data is yours. Algora will never sell user information. Members can access, export, or delete their data at any time, with full transparency about what is collected and why.

6

Community Governance

Our governance model empowers users to participate in shaping the platform’s rules, standards, and development. From policies to new features, members have a direct voice and vote (Scholz, 2017).

7

Fairness and Inclusion

Algora is designed for diversity and inclusivity, supporting meaningful engagement across cultural, social, and geographical divides. Everyone deserves a platform where they can contribute and be heard.

8

Sustainability

Digital futures must be environmentally responsible. Running massive data centers has a measurable carbon footprint, and we acknowledge this impact. Algora commits to energy-conscious hosting, efficient coding practices, and ethical technology partnerships to minimize harm to the planet, ensuring that building a fairer digital economy does not come at the expense of ecological health.

Our Pledge to the Community

Algora is more than a platform — it is a step toward a fairer, more transparent, and more human-centered digital economy. By valuing people over profits, empowering users to control their digital experiences, and ensuring that every contribution is recognized, we aim to create a new model for what social media can and should be.

— The Algora Team
