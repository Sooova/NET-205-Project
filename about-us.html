About Us

Meet the team building the future of social media

Our Story

At Algora, we are reimagining social media from the ground up. For too long, online platforms have thrived on a model that treats people’s creativity and attention as commodities to be extracted and sold. Every post, like, and share contributes to immense corporate profits, while the individuals who generate this value see little in return.

We believe it doesn’t have to be this way. Algora is a non-profit, community-owned social media platform built on principles of fairness, transparency, and respect for human creativity. Instead of exploiting user labor for advertising revenue, we reward members for the value they create. Instead of hiding the way your feed is shaped behind a black box, we put you in control. And instead of flooding your attention with AI-generated content, we prioritise authentic, human-made connections.

Our Mission

Reclaim your feed, own your content, and get paid for your voice. Welcome to Algora, a community-powered social platform designed for transparency, equity, and autonomy—free from algorithmic manipulation and profit-driven AI .

Our Core Values

Reclaiming Value

On today’s platforms, billions of users provide free labor—writing posts, creating videos, moderating conversations, and generating data that feeds advertising systems. While this digital labor fuels enormous profits for companies, it leaves contributors with nothing but fleeting engagement metrics. Algora exists to redress this imbalance by ensuring users are compensated for the value they generate.

Empowering Choice

Algorithms shape what we see online, yet most platforms keep their methods secret and tuned to maximize engagement, often at the expense of wellbeing. Algora gives the power back to users. Whether you prefer a chronological feed, an interest-driven one, or recommendations curated by your peers, you choose the algorithm that serves you best. Every algorithm is open, transparent, and explained in plain language.

Protecting Authenticity

We live in an age of generative AI, where synthetic content increasingly drowns out human creativity. Algora is different. We commit to a zero-AI content policy, ensuring that what you encounter on our platform comes from real people and communities, not machines programmed to hijack your attention.

Building Collective Ownership

Trust cannot exist without transparency. At Algora, our data practices, revenue models, and algorithms are fully open to public scrutiny. You’ll always know how your data is used, how recommendations are generated, and how funds are allocated. Our commitment is simple: no hidden agendas, no exploitation, no surprises.

Our Principles

1

People Before Profits

Algora is owned by its community, not investors. Our priority is creating a digital environment that enhances wellbeing, creativity, and fairness. Every decision is made with people—not profits—in mind.

2

Digital Labor Deserves Recognition

Content creation, moderation, and community-building are forms of labor. We acknowledge this labor and ensure that those who contribute are compensated through fair and transparent systems. The platform thrives because of its people, and they should share in its value.

3

Algorithmic Choice and Transparency

You deserve to understand and control how your feed is curated. We reject opaque algorithms designed to maximize engagement at all costs. Instead, Algora offers clear, open-source algorithms that you can select and adapt, ensuring your experience is shaped by your needs, not corporate interests.

4

Authentic Human Connection

We will never allow our platform to be overtaken by AI-generated content designed to mimic human expression. Algora is built to protect space for human creativity, dialogue, and community.

5

Data Sovereignty

Your data belongs to you. Algora will never sell user data, and every member has the right to access, export, or delete their information at any time. We operate with complete transparency regarding what data is collected, why it is collected, and how it is safeguarded.

6

Community Governance

Algora is not controlled from the top down. Our governance model empowers users to shape policies and platform design through collective decision-making. From feature development to community standards, members have a voice and a vote.

7

Fairness and Inclusion

We commit to building a platform that serves diverse communities and prioritises inclusivity. Algora is designed to support meaningful engagement across cultural, social, and geographical divides, fostering spaces where everyone has the opportunity to contribute and be heard.

8

Sustainability

Digital futures must also be environmentally sustainable. We strive to operate with energy-conscious hosting, ethical technology choices, and sustainable growth practices that minimize harm to the planet.

Our Pledge to the Community

Algora is more than a platform—it is a movement toward a fairer, more transparent, and more human-centered digital economy. By valuing people over profits, empowering users to control their digital experiences, and ensuring that every contribution is recognized, we aim to create a new model for what social media can and should be.

— The Algora Team
