Why Choose Algora?

A fundamentally different approach to social media that puts users first

The Algora Difference

Algora is a transformative social media platform offering an alternative to the revenue driven social media ecosystem of 2035. Algora is an AI free social media platform that allows users to choose their own algorithms, retain control over their data and participate in a transparent ethical online environment. Social media platforms continue to be governed by intensified privatisation and ever escalating ownership of digital platforms in our society (Lopez Solano, et al.,2022). Agora is the alternative in a social media environment saturated with big business dominating and controlling public opinion, workplaces and homes.

Rather than serving intrusive algorithmic content that controls how and what a user views, Algora promises algorithmic transparency. Users know exactly how their content feed is structured; what data is collected and the outcomes generated. In giving back the power for users to choose their own journey, Algora functions as a platform for people to create meaningful communities and experiences, with a focus on supporting its members.

Why choose Algora

Free from Ai manipulation

Algora delivers a truly authentic experience for users. AI manipulation increases risks such as misinformation amplification, deepfake circulation, and poses privacy concerns for users, in 2035 AI is dominating the social media environment. In creating an AI free environment Algora ensures the user is in control. AI has rapidly developed into a tool where business can collate and harness huge amounts of user data, concerns around information security and potential fake insights derived from AI are significant (Milan, Sahu, & Sandhu, 2023, p. 1753). Algora ensures that the user is engaging in a space where human judgement and choice remain central. Algora’s commitment to an AI free environment eliminates the risk of users being served AI generated misinformation and eliminates platform clutter.

Fair Compensation for content creators

Alogra is a game changer, by compensating creators for their contributions Algora recognises the intellectual labour, creativity and social energy that drive online communities. In 2035 user driven content creation continues to be at the core of how huge social media platforms ensure their platform thrives. Viral posts and popular creators yield immense value for platforms however most of that value has traditionally been captured by the platform owners. Content generated by a platform's user is the critical source of value in the social media landscape (Burgess, Marwick, & Poell, 2018, P.480) so it is therefore only fair that users are paid for their contributions. Algora has delivered this change.

Transparency and Trust

Algora is committed to algorithmic transparency. Every recommendation on the platform is open to inspection, being transparent allows for open feedback and revision of our algorithm policies. Users know exactly how their content feed is structured, what data is used and how outcomes are generated. This transparency builds trust, ensuring the platform functions as an asset for its community rather than a mechanism for exploiting its users. Unlike Facebook and Instagram who continue to be profit driven (Kim, 2017, p.153), Algora has been designed specifically for the users as a priority over profit.

Non Profit Community owned Model

The ownership structure of a social media platform has profound implications for its priorities, traditionally platforms have been focused on revenue expansion that has exploited and delivered platform delay to its users. Algora is a non-for-profit model reinvesting all revenue back into the platform to service improvements, infrastructure, compensate content creators and to deliver enhanced member benefits. The premise of developing a nonprofit platform is to achieve a mission, not to achieve profits (Pakroo, 2024, P.2), Algora is firmly focussed on fostering an environment that delivers user choice. The absence of investor pressure ensures that decision making aligns with ethical principles and community needs rather than exploiting members for profit margins.

Algora has delivered transparency, user rights and equitable compensation. The concept of ethical digital citizenship can be defined as ‘the use of knowledge and skills to behave ethically and responsibility in digital environments’ (Putri, Rahmawati & Zainudin, 2025, P.92). In 2035 Algora has delivered this environment for its community. Algora was developed in response to the demand for a platform free from online exploitation where users’ content has previously been commodified often without consent. Traditional platforms' appetite for data has fuelled heightened privacy concerns and reinforced a data centric form of capitalist behaviour (Srnicek, 2017, p.255). Algora is an engaging environment that respects the users right to choose their digital journey and treats its users as stakeholders not products.
